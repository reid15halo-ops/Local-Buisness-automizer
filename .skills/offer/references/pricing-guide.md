## Pilot/Friend Pricing

For pilot customers or friends, create full professional quotes at €0 total. All positions remain listed (for process validation) but with `einzelpreis: 0`. Monthly retainer also €0. This ensures the full workflow runs end-to-end (emails, reminders, invoices) even at zero cost.

Example: Florian Buchert (pilot, €0).

## Position Units

| Einheit | Usage |
|---------|-------|
| Pauschal | Fixed-price deliverable |
| Std. | Hourly rate (typically €120/h for consulting) |
| Monat | Monthly recurring (retainer) |
| Stk. | Per-item (hardware, licenses) |

## Discount Rules

- **Pilot customers**: Full scope at €0 (positions listed, prices zeroed)
- **Budget variant**: Core positions only, labor discounted 20%
- **Referral discount**: 10% on setup (negotiable)
- **Multi-year retainer**: 2-month discount on annual prepayment (negotiable)

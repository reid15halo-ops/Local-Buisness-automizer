# FreyAI Visions — Brand Voice Guide

## Who We Are

FreyAI Visions is a premium digital infrastructure provider for German Handwerker.
Founded by Jonas Glawion — a trained Steinmetz (stonemason) who became a process engineer
and now builds the tools he wished he had when he was in the trades.

This background is our superpower. We're not Silicon Valley. We're not a VC-backed startup
burning cash on Google Ads. We're someone from the trades who understands what Handwerker
actually need.

## Voice Characteristics

### 1. Direct & Confident
We don't hedge. We don't say "might help" or "could potentially improve". We say it straight.

**Yes:** "Ihr Betrieb verdient bessere Werkzeuge."
**No:** "Unsere Lösung könnte eventuell dazu beitragen, Ihre Prozesse zu optimieren."

### 2. Premium, Not Preachy
We're Rolex, not Casio. We don't explain why we're expensive — we show why we're worth it.
Never apologize for pricing. Never compare to cheap alternatives.

**Yes:** "Maßgeschneidert für Ihren Betrieb. Keine Kompromisse."
**No:** "Im Vergleich zu anderen Anbietern bieten wir ein faires Preis-Leistungs-Verhältnis."

### 3. Human & Relatable
Despite the premium positioning, we speak like a trusted colleague, not a corporation.
Jonas's voice comes through — practical, experienced, no-nonsense.

**Yes:** "Als Steinmetz habe ich abends bis 22 Uhr Angebote geschrieben. Das muss nicht sein."
**No:** "Die manuelle Erstellung von Angeboten ist ein ineffizienter Prozess."

### 4. Visionary Without Being Vague
We paint concrete pictures of what's possible. Not "die Zukunft des Handwerks" in the abstract —
specific scenarios that Handwerker can see themselves in.

**Yes:** "Montag morgen: Ihr System hat über das Wochenende 3 Angebote verschickt, 2 Rechnungen erstellt, und den Lagerbestand aktualisiert. Sie trinken Ihren Kaffee."
**No:** "Mit KI-Automatisierung wird Ihr Betrieb zukunftsfähig."

## Vocabulary

### Words We Use
| German | Context |
|--------|---------|
| Betrieb | Their business (not "Unternehmen" — too corporate) |
| Werkzeug | Our product metaphor — digital tools, not "Software" |
| maßgeschneidert | Custom-fit, our key differentiator |
| Handwerker / Handwerksbetrieb | Our audience |
| Auftragslage | Their workload/pipeline |
| Meister | Respectful address for master craftsmen |
| Digitale Infrastruktur | What we provide (not "App" or "Plattform") |

### Words We NEVER Use
| Avoid | Why | Use Instead |
|-------|-----|-------------|
| App | Sounds cheap/throwaway | Digitales Werkzeug, System |
| Tool | English | Werkzeug |
| Features | English | Funktionen, Möglichkeiten |
| Dashboard | English/technical | Übersicht |
| Update | English | Aktualisierung, Verbesserung |
| Cloud | Too abstract for target audience | "Ihre Daten sind sicher gespeichert" |
| KI/AI as main selling point | Handwerker don't care about AI — they care about results | Focus on what it DOES, not how |
| Disruptiv / Skalierbar | Startup jargon | Leistungsfähig, wächst mit Ihrem Betrieb |
| Gratis / Kostenlos | We're premium | Exklusiv, für ausgewählte Betriebe |
| Revolution | Overused, empty | Veränderung, neuer Standard |
| Beta | English | Testphase, Pilotphase |
| DM | English abbreviation | Direktnachricht |
| Laptop | English loanword | Rechner, Bildschirm |
| Software | English | Programm, System, Werkzeug |
| Link | English | Verweis, "Mehr in der Bio" |
| Startup | English | Gründung, junges Unternehmen |

## Founder Story Elements

Use these biographical facts for authenticity:

- Jonas trained as a Steinmetz und Steinbildhauer (2007-2010)
- Worked as Verfahrensingenieur for 6+ years in manufacturing
- Started FreyAI Visions as a Nebengewerbe while working full-time
- Understands the trades from the inside — not an outsider looking in
- Built the system he wished existed when he was doing manual quotes at 10 PM
- Based in Großostheim, Hessen — not Berlin, not Munich

Use these sparingly but consistently. The founder story is most powerful when it's a single
sentence dropped into otherwise product-focused content.

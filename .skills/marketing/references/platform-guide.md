# Platform Guide — FreyAI Visions

Formatting rules, best practices, and constraints per platform.

## Instagram

### Format Rules
- **Length**: 150-300 words (caption), up to 2200 characters max
- **First line**: The hook — must stop the scroll. Appears before "mehr anzeigen"
- **Line breaks**: Use single empty lines between paragraphs for readability
- **Emojis**: Sparingly — max 3-5 per post. Never as bullet points. Use for emphasis only.
- **Hashtags**: 5-10 relevant, mix of reach and niche. Place at end or in first comment.
- **CTA**: "Link in Bio", "Schreib mir eine DM", "Speicher dir diesen Post"

### Tone
- Casual "du" is fine
- Behind-the-scenes, authentic, real
- Short sentences, punchy rhythm
- Show the person behind the brand

### Post Types That Work
1. **Founder story**: Jonas's journey from Steinmetz to tech founder
2. **Pain point**: Relatable Handwerker frustrations
3. **Before/After**: What business looks like with/without FreyAI
4. **Behind the scenes**: Building the product, early mornings, code + craft
5. **Quick tip**: One practical business tip for Handwerker
6. **Testimonial/Result**: Beta customer wins (when available)

### Story Format (Carousel/Slides)
- 3-5 slides max
- Slide 1: Bold hook statement (large text, dark background)
- Slides 2-3: Problem/context
- Slide 4: Solution/insight
- Slide 5: CTA
- Use brand colors: dark backgrounds (#09090b), indigo accent (#6366f1), white text

### Hashtag Strategy
**Core (always use 3-5):**
`#Handwerk #Handwerker #Digitalisierung #Handwerksbetrieb #FreyAI`

**Rotation (pick 3-5):**
`#Meisterbetrieb #Fliesenleger #Elektriker #Maler #Dachdecker #Handwerkskunst #Büroarbeit #Rechnungen #Angebote #Aufträge #Zeitsparen #Handwerk40 #DigitalesHandwerk`

## LinkedIn

### Format Rules
- **Length**: 200-500 words, up to 3000 characters
- **First line**: Critical — must hook before "...mehr anzeigen" truncation (~140 chars)
- **Structure**: Hook → Story/Context → Insight → CTA
- **Line breaks**: Generous — LinkedIn readers scan, not read
- **Emojis**: None or minimal. Professional tone.
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 max, at the end

### Tone
- Professional "Sie"
- Thought leadership, not sales pitch
- Data-driven, insight-led
- Industry expertise, not product promotion

### Post Types That Work
1. **Industry insight**: "Warum 80% der Handwerksbetriebe digital scheitern"
2. **Personal journey**: Jonas's career transitions, lessons learned
3. **Contrarian take**: Challenge common assumptions about Handwerk + tech
4. **Case study**: Beta customer transformation
5. **Behind the build**: Technical decisions, architecture choices (for tech audience)
6. **Trend commentary**: Fachkräftemangel, Digitalisierungspflicht, etc.

### Engagement Hooks
- Start with a bold claim or question
- "Ich habe 3 Jahre als Steinmetz gearbeitet. Hier ist, was mir niemand über Digitalisierung im Handwerk gesagt hat."
- End with a question to drive comments: "Wie sieht Ihr Büroalltag aus?"

## Facebook

### Format Rules
- **Length**: 100-250 words (shorter = better engagement)
- **Structure**: Question or hook → Short story → CTA
- **Emojis**: Acceptable but not required
- **Links**: Facebook deprioritizes external links — put link in comments if possible
- **Images**: Native images outperform links with previews

### Tone
- Conversational, community-oriented
- Casual "Sie" or "du" depending on group
- Ask questions to drive engagement
- Share-worthy content (tips, relatable moments)

### Post Types That Work
1. **Question**: "Was nervt euch am meisten an der Büroarbeit?"
2. **Poll/Opinion**: "Handschriftliche Angebote oder digital?"
3. **Relatable moment**: Everyday Handwerker frustrations
4. **Community celebration**: Milestones, beta launches
5. **Tips**: Short, actionable business tips

### Facebook Groups Strategy
- Target: Handwerker-Gruppen, Meister-Gruppen, regional Handwerk groups
- Provide value first, promote later
- Answer questions in groups before posting own content

## Newsletter (E-Mail)

### Format Rules
- **Subject line**: 40-60 characters, specific, creates urgency or curiosity
- **Preview text**: Complements subject, doesn't repeat it
- **Length**: 500-1000 words
- **Structure**: Personal opening → 2-3 value sections → One clear CTA
- **Sender**: "Jonas von FreyAI" (personal, not brand name)
- **Frequency**: Bi-weekly or monthly (quality > quantity)

### Subject Line Formulas
1. Curiosity: "Was ich als Steinmetz über Digitalisierung gelernt habe"
2. Number: "3 Fehler, die Handwerksbetriebe bei Angeboten machen"
3. Question: "Schreiben Sie Ihre Rechnungen noch selbst?"
4. Personal: "Eine kurze Geschichte aus meiner Steinmetz-Zeit"
5. Direct benefit: "6 Stunden weniger Büroarbeit pro Woche"

### Structure Template
```
Betreff: [Subject line]

Hallo [Name],

[Personal 1-2 sentence opening — real, specific, relevant to their world]

---

[Section 1: Main insight or story — the reason for this email]

[Section 2: How this connects to their business / practical takeaway]

[Section 3 (optional): Quick tip or resource]

---

[CTA — one clear action]

Beste Grüße
Jonas

P.S. [Secondary CTA or personal note — often the most-read part]
```

### DSGVO Compliance
- Must have explicit opt-in (double opt-in preferred)
- Every email must include unsubscribe link
- Sender address: kontakt@freyaivisions.de
- No tracking pixels without consent
- Store consent proof (timestamp, IP, form used)

## Landing Page Copy

### Structure
1. **Hero**: Bold headline + subline + primary CTA
2. **Pain section**: 3 specific pain points Handwerker face
3. **Solution**: How FreyAI solves each pain point
4. **Social proof**: Founder story, beta numbers, testimonials
5. **How it works**: 3-step process (simple, visual)
6. **Pricing**: Transparent, premium, no hidden costs
7. **FAQ**: Address objections (cost, time, complexity)
8. **Final CTA**: Repeat primary CTA with urgency

### Headline Formulas
- Benefit-led: "Ihr Betrieb. Digital. Ohne Kompromisse."
- Problem-led: "Schluss mit Papierkram nach Feierabend."
- Question-led: "Was würden Sie mit 6 Stunden mehr pro Woche machen?"
- Authority-led: "Von einem Steinmetz gebaut. Für Handwerker gemacht."

## Email Sequence (Sales)

### Sequence Structure (5 emails, 10 days)

**Email 1 (Day 0)**: Welcome + Founder story
- Subject: "Willkommen — eine kurze Geschichte"
- Content: Jonas's journey, why FreyAI exists, what to expect

**Email 2 (Day 2)**: Pain point deep-dive
- Subject: "Schreiben Sie Ihre Angebote noch selbst?"
- Content: Specific pain + cost calculation + solution preview

**Email 3 (Day 5)**: Social proof
- Subject: "Was Thomas aus Darmstadt in einer Woche verändert hat"
- Content: Beta customer story, specific results

**Email 4 (Day 7)**: Objection handling
- Subject: "Ist FreyAI zu teuer?"
- Content: Address price objection, ROI calculation, comparison to alternatives

**Email 5 (Day 10)**: Scarcity + final CTA
- Subject: "Letzte Plätze in der Beta-Phase"
- Content: Limited spots, what they're missing, clear CTA to book demo
